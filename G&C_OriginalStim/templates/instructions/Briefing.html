Instructions

In this experiment, you will see pictures of two sets of stimuli.

There will be two parts of the experiment, each containing a learning phase and an assessment phase.

During the learning phase you will be presented with an image and be asked to classify it into one of three categories. The computer will tell you what the correct category is for each object.

After the learning phase there will be a assessment phase. During the assessment phase the computer will no longer provide any feedback.
