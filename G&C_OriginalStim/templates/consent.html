You are invited to participate in a research study about how people learn to categorize items.

What the study is about: The purpose of this study is to better understand the way in which people pay attention to various dimensions when categorizing objects.

What we will ask you to do: If you agree to participate, you will complete a 20-25 minute computerized procedure in which you will learn to place stimuli into one of three categories, and then your learning wil be tested in an assessment phase. You will have 30 minutes to complete this task.

Compensation: You will receive $1.20 as compensation for participating in this study. An additional $1.00 bonus will be rewarded to the top %10 performing participants. You must complete the entire task to recieve this bonus and will be contacted when all 150 participants have completed their task.

Taking part is voluntary: Participation in this study is completely voluntary. If you decide to take part, you are free to withdraw at any time.

Your answers will be confidential. The records of this study will be kept private. In any sort of report we make public, we will not include any information that will make it possible to identify you.
